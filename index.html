Empowerment

In coaching, we focus on the present and the future, instead of dwelling on the past. We uncover any thoughts you are having, even the ones you weren't aware of, that cause you to feel and act a certain way. When you recognize these thoughts, you can decide if those thoughts are serving you or hurting you and if you want to keep them.
