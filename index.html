It is so easy to say, "I am frustrated!" But what does that even feel like or mean? Sometimes it is just something we say out of habit, and not reality. I would like to suggest that instead, you stop and tell yourself to take a deep breath and then decide how you feel. It still might be frustrated, but often it is something different. There is power in figuring out your emotions and naming them as you feel them.

MY WORK

In coaching, we focus on the present and the future, instead of dwelling on the past. We uncover any thoughts you are having, even the ones you weren't aware of, that cause you to feel and act a certain way. When you recognize these thoughts, you can decide if those thoughts are serving you or hurting you and if you want to keep them. I create a safe space to sort through your thoughts and be aware of how your thoughts create your feelings. I give you tools to help you take your life from good to great! Want to give it a try?
